Visualize Spatio Temporal data

In this project, we visualize the changes in total population per district in the City of Münster over the past 20 years — open data from the city administration of Münster. Knowing where the population increases over the period, which parts require planning and construction, and how long the people stay can help planners and administrators choose the right plan for the city.
